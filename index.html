Full Spa Day Package

Colour Therapy Experience

Colour affects how you feel — this is scientifically established. Our colour therapy sessions use specific light wavelengths to calm anxiety, shift your mood, and encourage deep nervous system relaxation. Gentle, non-invasive, and genuinely effective.

  • Mood elevation and anxiety reduction
  • Non-invasive, fully clothed experience
  • Pairs beautifully with massage or facial treatments
04

Massage Therapy

More than relaxation — these massages are health-focused. Our therapists work with your body, not just on it, addressing chronic tension, muscle recovery, and nervous system regulation. Swedish, deep tissue, and therapeutic wellness options available.

  • Chronic tension and muscle pain relief
  • Post-exercise recovery and circulation support
  • Stress hormone reduction and sleep improvement
05

Beauty & Wellness Treatments

Facials, skin care, and body treatments designed to nourish from the outside in. Whether targeting a specific skin concern, maintaining your glow, or simply indulging — we have the treatment that fits where you are right now.

  • Targeted facials for your skin type and concerns
  • Professional-grade products and techniques
  • Results you see and skin you feel confident in
